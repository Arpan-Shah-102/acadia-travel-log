Blog #3: The explanation of the cost.

The cost of the gas for the whole trip is $309.87. This is because we took into consideration of the average MPG of a standard car. Then, we divided the round trip distance from Campbellsville to Acadia by the MPG of the car to get how many gallons we need. Lastly, we multiplied that by the average gas price (~$3.13) to get the final cost.
